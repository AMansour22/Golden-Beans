About

Golden Beans is an Egyptian company specializing in the field of nuts, with a particular focus on the production and distribution of pine nuts. The company owns a factory in Turkey that has been operating in this field for over 60 years, gaining extensive experience and achieving significant success in exporting pine nuts to various global markets.
